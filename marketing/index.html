Features

Everything you need
at the front desk

👁

Live detection

Continuous scanning at ~2 frames per second. Coloured bounding boxes identify known customers, employees, and unknowns at a glance.

📸

Instant ID scan

Single-tap identification of everyone in frame. One detection token, results in under a second — perfect for quieter moments.

🏅

Customer tiers

Assign Standard, Silver, Gold, Platinum, or VIP tiers to customers. Tier colour appears on every bounding box so staff know the priority instantly.

📡

Live streams

Broadcast the front desk feed to any back-office device. Managers and hosts see the same live overlay — faces, names, tiers — without a camera of their own.

📱

Mobile & wearables

Full feature set on iOS and Android. Connect smart glasses and use a hands-free first-person view while results display on your phone.

🏢

Multi-desk corporate

Unlimited users sharing one customer database. Add a new front desk in minutes — no configuration, no syncing, just log in.

How it works

Up and running
in minutes

1

Sign up & set up

Create a free account at crowdview.tv. No downloads. Open the Hub Screen in Chrome on any PC with a webcam.

Point the camera at your door. Unknown faces appear in red — click any to add a name, notes, and tier. They're enrolled instantly.
