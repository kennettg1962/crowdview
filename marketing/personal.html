Features

Your personal
face memory

🧠

Instant recall

Point your camera at anyone you've met before. CrowdView surfaces their name, notes, and context in under a second — so you can greet them confidently.

📝

Rich notes

Add anything when you enrol someone — where you met, what they do, mutual connections, topics to bring up. It's all waiting the next time you see them.

📸

Photo library

Store multiple photos per person for better recognition accuracy. CrowdView handles varied lighting, angles, and expressions automatically.

🎙

Voice commands

Hands-free operation in Chrome. Say "scan" to identify faces or "stop" to pause — perfect when your hands are otherwise occupied at events.

📱

Phone & wearables

Use your phone camera on the move, or connect smart glasses for a true hands-free first-person view with results in your ear.

🕶 AR glasses: compatible with INMO Air 3 only

🔒

Private by design

Your face database is yours alone. Nobody else can see the people you've enrolled — your contacts, notes, and recognition data are completely private.

How it works

Ready before
the next handshake

1

Create a free account

Sign up at crowdview.tv in under a minute. No download, no credit card. Open in Chrome on any device with a camera.

2

Enrol people you know

Take a photo or upload one. Add their name, notes, and context. Repeat for anyone you want to remember — it takes about 30 seconds per person.

3

Let CrowdView do the rest

At your next event, open the app and start Live mode. Known faces are identified instantly — name and notes right there on screen.

Use cases

Made for real
human connection

🧩

Face blindness & prosopagnosia

An estimated 2.5% of people have difficulty recognising faces — a condition called prosopagnosia. CrowdView acts as a discreet, always-available prosthetic memory: identify colleagues, friends, and family with confidence and never be caught in that awful moment of not recognising someone you know.

🤝

Networking & conferences

You've shaken a hundred hands today. CrowdView remembers all of them. Spot someone across the room and instantly recall their name, company, and what you talked about — follow-ups become effortless.

🎉

Social events & parties

Know you've met someone but can't place them? A quick glance at your phone puts the name right there. Walk into any social situation with the confidence to greet everyone by name.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family gatherings & reunions

Extended family, distant cousins, relatives you see once a decade — CrowdView quietly prompts you so you're never caught asking "which one are you again?" at the family reunion.

🏥

Memory & caregiving support

Support loved ones who struggle with recognition due to age, illness, or injury. CrowdView can be a gentle, discreet aid for people navigating memory challenges in daily life.
